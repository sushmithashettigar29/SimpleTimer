About SimpleTimer

Our Story

SimpleTimer was born out of a simple need - to create the most user-friendly, reliable online stopwatch that works seamlessly across all devices. Founded in 2025, our mission has been to provide a timing tool that's both powerful and incredibly simple to use.

Why Choose SimpleTimer?

  • No Installation Needed - Access directly from your browser
  • Works Offline - Once loaded, works without internet
  • Cross-Platform - Perfect on desktop, tablet, or mobile
  • Zero Cost - Completely free with no hidden charges
  • Privacy Focused - We don't track or store your data
